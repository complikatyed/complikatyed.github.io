From English Teacher to Web Developer

I came to coding when, as a 7th grade English teacher, I realized that designing and creating technology tools had taken over the bulk of my prep time (and become the most fascinating aspect of my job). While I miss the excitement of my middle school students, I'm completely in love with the many-faceted challenges of full-stack coding.

Incorporating technology in my classroom:
  • Paired with USN Technology Coordinator to create and teach tech/literature integrated classes.
  • Piloted exclusive use of Google Docs for all writing assignments.
  • Used Google documents to monitor and guide students during the writing process
  • Built an extensive class website to provide information for students, parents, and tutors
  • Created web-based assessments and practice activities for literature, grammar, and vocabulary
  • Assisted colleagues with creation and maintenance of their own teacher web pages
